How to connect your account

  1. Enter your Riot Games summoner name in the format: GameName#Tag
  2. For example: PlayerName#NA1 or MyUsername#EUW
  3. Choose whether to set this as your primary account
  4. Click "Connect Account" to link it to your profile

Note: Your summoner name must be exactly as it appears in-game, including the tag line (the part after the #).

Need help finding your summoner name?

1. Open League of Legends

Launch the League of Legends client and log in to your account.

2. Check your profile

Your summoner name and tag are displayed in the top right corner of the client.

3. Copy the full name

Make sure to include both the name and the tag (e.g., "PlayerName#NA1").

Common Issues:

  • Make sure you include the # and tag line
  • Check for typos in your summoner name
  • Ensure your account is on the North American server (NA1)
  • If you recently changed your name, wait a few minutes and try again
